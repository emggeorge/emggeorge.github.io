Manifesto

2025-10-21

Writing this feels a little like changing with my window open: no one and everyone is watching at the same time.

When I've sat down to write this, I've felt overwhelmed by need to write something worth reading. My relationship with writing is long and deep; I've been keeping a diary since 2014, but the strength of a diary is what holds me back in this moment. To cross the threshold from private to public is something I don't know how to do.

So I'm moving slowly. This blog will move slowly. It already has, taking me many months to go from playground to finished object. I am at a part of my life, though, where I want to move slowly. Not slow in a lazy way, but slow like a tsunami, working my way across the ocean, collecting energy and matter, so that someday, when I am ready, I may crash with my full self into the shore.

That's what why I'm writing this blog and setting up this website. It's the corner where I can tend to the public parts of myself, exposing what I believe in to the risks that come with being seen and hoping that the process changes me.
